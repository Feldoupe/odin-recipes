Chicken Tikka Masala

What is it?

Chicken Tikka Masala is a popular Indian dish featuring grilled marinated chicken chunks (tikka) in a creamy, spiced tomato sauce. The sauce, rich in flavor, is made with tomatoes, cream, and a blend of Indian spices. It is typically served with rice or naan bread.

Ingredients

Chicken Tikka ingredients

  • 500g boneless chicken breast
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 tsp ground cumin
  • 2 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp ground coriander
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 2 tsp garam masala
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 inch ginger, grated

Sauce ingredients

  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 inch ginger, grated
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 can (400g) tomato puree
  • 200ml heavy cream
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Chicken Tikka Recipe - Step by step

  1. Cut the chicken into bite-sized pieces. In a bowl, mix yogurt, lemon juice, cumin, paprika, coriander, turmeric, garam masala, garlic, and ginger. Add the chicken and marinate for at least 1 hour.
  2. Preheat the oven to 200°C (400°F). Thread the marinated chicken onto skewers and bake for 20-25 minutes, until cooked through. Set aside.
  3. In a large pan, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat. Sauté the onions until golden brown.
  4. Add the garlic, ginger, cumin, paprika, and garam masala to the pan, cooking for another minute until fragrant.
  5. Stir in the tomato puree, bring to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for 10 minutes.
  6. Add the cooked chicken to the sauce and stir well. Pour in the cream and simmer for an additional 10 minutes.
  7. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot with rice or naan.

Sauce recipe - Step by step

  1. In a large pan, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat. Sauté the onions until golden brown.
  2. Add the garlic, ginger, cumin, paprika, and garam masala to the pan, cooking for another minute until fragrant.
  3. Stir in the tomato puree, bring to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Pour in the cream and simmer for an additional 10 minutes until the sauce thickens.
  5. Add the cooked chicken to the sauce and stir well. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot.
